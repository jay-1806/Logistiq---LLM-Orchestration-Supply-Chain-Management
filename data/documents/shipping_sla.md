# Shipping SLA Policy

## 1. Customer Tier Definitions

| Tier | Description | Examples |
|------|------------|---------|
| Platinum | Top-revenue accounts with contractual SLAs | DataVault Inc, CloudNine Systems |
| Gold | High-volume repeat customers | Acme Corp, GlobalTech |
| Silver | Standard commercial accounts | MegaStore, TechFlow Ltd |

## 2. Shipping Timelines by Priority

### Standard Priority
- **Platinum**: Ship within 7 business days of order creation
- **Gold**: Ship within 10 business days
- **Silver**: Ship within 14 business days

### Expedited Priority
- **Platinum**: Ship within 3 business days
- **Gold**: Ship within 5 business days  
- **Silver**: Ship within 7 business days

### Critical Priority
- **All tiers**: Ship within 2 business days
- Requires VP-level approval for resource allocation
- Triggers automatic daily status reporting to customer

## 3. SLA Breach Consequences

| Breach Type | Consequence |
|------------|-------------|
| 1-2 days late | Customer notification + internal escalation |
| 3-5 days late | Discount on current order (5-10%) + root cause analysis |
| 5+ days late | Executive escalation + potential contract penalty |

## 4. Expedite Approval Criteria

An order may be upgraded to expedited priority if ALL of the following are true:

1. Customer has made the request directly (documented in order notes)
2. Current inventory can fulfill the order without impacting other critical orders
3. Carrier capacity is available for the requested timeline
4. The additional shipping cost is either:
   - Absorbed by the customer, OR
   - Approved by a Supply Chain Manager (for strategic accounts)

## 5. Carrier Selection

| Carrier | Use Case | Transit Time (Domestic) |
|---------|---------|----------------------|
| FedEx Priority | Critical/Expedited shipments | 1-2 days |
| UPS Ground | Standard shipments | 3-5 days |
| DHL Express | International shipments | 3-7 days |
| Freight (LTL) | Bulk orders > 500 units | 5-10 days |
